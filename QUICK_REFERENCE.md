## How to Play
1. **Open** `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Type** the green words/phrases to shoot enemies
3. **Defeat** the specified number of enemies to face the boss
4. **Beat** the boss word to level up
5. **Progress** through increasingly difficult levels

## Game Tips

1. **Focus on fast typing** - Speed is key to staying ahead of enemies
2. **Use powerups wisely** - Save bombs for when overwhelmed
3. **Watch the boss** - It stays at the top, easier to target
4. **Stay centered** - Keeps you roughly equidistant from spawns
5. **Build combos** - Consecutive kills increase score multiplier
